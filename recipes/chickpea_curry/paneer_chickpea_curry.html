Chickpea Curry

Home Recipes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 12oz Paneer, 2 lb Chicken, or None
  • 2 15.5oz can Chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup Cream (or 3/4 if you want it more liquidy for breads)
  • 6 oz spinach
  • 4 tsp (1.33 tbsp) Vandouvan Curry Powder
  • 2 8oz Tomato Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Yellow Curry Paste
  • Naan/Rice/None

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (175°C).
  2. Mix cream, curry powder, tomato sauce, and curry paste in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Add spinach, chickpeas, and paneer (if using). Mix well.
  4. Pour mixture into a large baking pan.
  5. If using chicken, add chicken on top of the mixture in the large baking pan.
  6. Bake 34 minutes.
  7. Once done baking, server over rice or with Naan.
